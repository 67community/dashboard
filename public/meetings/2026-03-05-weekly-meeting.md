## Key Tips from Weston

- **Reverse prompting**: Don't know how to prompt? Ask AI to ask *you* questions and extract the info itself — then it prompts itself. Massive unlock.
- **Skills**: Automations built by talking to Claude. Example: "launch invoice skill" → AI reviews last 10 invoices and sends follow-up emails. Can be scheduled (e.g. every day at 7am).
- **Connectors**: Bridges to Gmail, Calendar, etc. — AI can make changes directly without opening a browser.
- **Warning**: Don't download skills from random sources online — malware risk. Build your own.
